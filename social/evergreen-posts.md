## 10. Why most developers are building the wrong way for AI agents
**Format:** 3-tweet mini-thread
**Expiry:** Evergreen

1/ Most developers are building their APIs the same way they have for 15 years.

Dashboard. Email verification. Credit card. Rate limits.

That's fine. Until agents are your primary customer.

2/ Agents don't have inboxes. They can't click verification links. They don't have credit cards.

Every API that requires human-mediated signup is locked out of the fastest-growing category of software buyers.

3/ The fix isn't technically hard. It's a design decision.

One POST endpoint that returns API key + wallet address + billing info.

The developers who make this decision now will be infrastructure. The ones who wait will retrofit it when it's already too late.
